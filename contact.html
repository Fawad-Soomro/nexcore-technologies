Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Typical websites take 2-4 weeks from start to finish. Complex e-commerce sites may take 4-8 weeks. Timeline depends on project scope and your feedback speed.

What are your pricing rates?

We offer competitive, transparent pricing starting from $500 for basic websites. Each project is unique, so we provide custom quotes based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide hosting and domain services?

Yes! We offer reliable web hosting and domain registration services. We can also work with your existing hosting provider if you prefer.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. Mobile-first design is our standard.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes, we provide maintenance and support packages starting from $50/month. This includes updates, security monitoring, and technical assistance.

Can you help with SEO and digital marketing?

Yes! We offer SEO optimization, Google Analytics setup, and can connect you with trusted marketing partners for comprehensive digital strategies.
